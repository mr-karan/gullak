Gullak is a local-first expense tracker. Your ledger lives on your phone, not in someone else's cloud. It works fully offline, with no account and no server required. Sync and AI are optional extras that you run yourself.

<b>Fast to log</b>

Type an amount and save in a few taps. Or describe a spend in plain words and let AI fill in the fields. Snap a receipt and it reads the total. Splits, transfers, tags, and a system share-sheet target are all built in.

<b>See where your money goes</b>

Daily and monthly trends, spending by category, and a spend heatmap — drawn with a lightweight in-app chart kit, no heavy dependencies. Set monthly budget envelopes and watch per-category progress rings fill as you spend.

<b>Bank SMS become drafts you approve (optional)</b>

Gullak can turn bank SMS into draft transactions you review and confirm in one tap. This is opt-in and requires the SMS permission. Please note:

- The app works fully without it. If you never grant SMS access, nothing about SMS runs.
- When enabled, messages are parsed on a sync server that YOU configure and control (self-hosted) using an AI model that YOU configure. There is no Gullak-operated cloud in this path.
- Nothing is uploaded to any third party. There are no analytics or trackers.
- You can disable SMS capture, or revoke the permission, at any time.

<b>Optional self-hosted server</b>

Run the open-source sync server on your own hardware to sync across devices, hold your own AI keys, chat with a conversational assistant (in-app or over WhatsApp), and mirror activity to Google Sheets or Actual Budget. The phone stays the source of truth; the server is just a merge point.

<b>Private by design</b>

No trackers. No analytics. No ad networks. No cloud you didn't choose. Gullak is free and open source.
